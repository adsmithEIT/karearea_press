Copyright Statement


This website was created as assessment for the web development course ITWD5.130 2024 Semester 2 at EIT, Hawke’s Bay, New Zealand.


Due to a lack of the resources required to create a unique catalogue, the displayed books are from the New Zealand publisher, Potton and Burton; with the exception of the following: Mystical Mushrooms and Forager’s Treasury from Allen and Unwin, Fungi of Aotearoa from Penguin, and Know Your NZ Native Plants from Upstart Press.


Much of the code was adopted from the ITWD5.130 Moodle site, as well as from w3schools.com.


Banner image from "Enraptored," by M. Bathurst, 2009, New Zealand National Geographic, Issue 098 (https://www.nzgeo.com/stories/enraptored/).
