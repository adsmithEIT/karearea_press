About Us


We are a small publishing company working to increase knowledge and awareness of New Zealand’s unique wildlife and environment. Our books draw on extensive research and evidence collected in the field. While being well informed by scientific literature, they are not dry academic tomes. Our titles are highly readable and engaging with extensive full colour photography and illustrations.
